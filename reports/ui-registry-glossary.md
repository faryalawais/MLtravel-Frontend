## Why this exists

Per the Executable-Requirements PRD (`tokens/templates/PRD-Executable-Requirements-Gherkin-Component-Paths.docx.md`), every UI element referenced from a Jira ticket — and from every Gherkin scenario — uses a **stable path** instead of a description. `component.checkout.cart.checkoutButton` is the contract; the visual, the position, and the generated test-id are not.
